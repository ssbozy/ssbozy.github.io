About

Friends and family call me Sandy. During my day, I work at Adobe. What I do the rest of the time is a mystery. I live in the almost beautiful side of the SF Bay Area with my young family.

I am a hacker by nature and that means finding roundabout solutions to any problem let it be in programming, software/hardware development or life in general. I believe we can always find a simple solution which works. As humans, we tend to be attracted to complexity.

Someone once told me to fall in love with problems and not solutions. This made me keep refining every solution until I think it is simple to explain. I re-visit every problem to check if it still a problem.
